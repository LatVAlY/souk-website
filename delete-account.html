Request Account Deletion

To delete your Souk account, please follow these steps in the mobile app:

  1. Open the Souk app on your device.
  2. Log in with your account credentials.
  3. Go to Settings from the main menu.
  4. Scroll down and select Delete Account.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your account deletion.

Note: Account deletion is only available through the Souk mobile app for your security. If you need further assistance, please contact our support team.
